About San Jose Home

A Haven for Senior Citizens

San Jose Home presents a beautiful haven for families concerned about their elderly parents living alone, by offering assisted living, comprehensive personal care addressing their physical, spiritual, and emotional needs. The contemporary economic and social environment often necessitates dual-income households, compelling both spouses to work. This situation can lead to concerns regarding the safety, security, and well-being of elderly parents in the absence of their children. San Jose Home provides a perfect solution for these families, offering a secure, comfortable, and homely atmosphere combined with personalized care. The necessity for such care becomes even more pronounced for elderly individuals who do not receive adequate attention due to various reasons, including poverty, homelessness, insufficient income, and the absence of their children during critical times. At San Jose Home, the assisted living facilities and compassionate care enables the senior citizens to overcome loneliness, insecurity, and health issues, attain peace and happiness with confidence. San Jose Home, managed by the Assisi Sisters of Mary Immaculate, is committed to supporting the senior citizens a secure, safe and comfortable living environment, caring for all their needs. The home's mission is to provide a nurturing and secure haven for the elderly, thereby fostering an atmosphere of dignity, respect, and comprehensive care for all residents, regardless of their social or economic status

Our Legacy of Care

A Mission of Compassion

Our Foundation

San Jose Home, nestled in Cheenikuzhy, near Thodupuzha, Kerala, is a dedicated project by the Assisi Sisters of Mary Immaculate (A.S.M.I.), St. Joseph's Province. Established with the vision of providing a safe and secure living environment for senior citizens, our home represents the congregation's commitment to compassionate elderly care.

Our Heritage

The A.S.M.I., founded by Servant of God Msgr. Joseph Kandathil on April 2, 1949, has a rich legacy of humanitarian service. Our congregation's mission extends across various healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and social service centers, serving the most vulnerable in society.

Our Comprehensive Care Network

The Assisi Sisters serve in diverse healthcare settings including general hospitals, leprosy hospitals and asylums, homes for the aged, cancer care facilities, HIV-AIDS centers, TB clinics, and rehabilitation centers for the disabled. Our educational initiatives encompass nursing colleges, schools for underprivileged children, and special education facilities for the deaf, blind, and mentally challenged.

Beyond healthcare and education, our sisters are deeply involved in evangelization, social work, and parish ministry. This holistic approach reflects our unwavering commitment to community development and support, ensuring dignity and comprehensive care for all those we serve.

OUR PURPOSE

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To Facilitate the deserving senior citizens to lead lives marked by dignity, self-esteem and independence, by offering a secure, compassionate and nurturing environment.

Our Mission

To share compassionate love and care to the senior citizens, encouraging hope and promoting companionship to achieve mental, physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. Personalized care by experienced professionals, combined with nutritious food and opportunities for physical and spiritual growth, ensures that the lives of senior citizens are comfortable and enriched.

Fr. Jose Varickamackal

Spiritual Director & Founder

Our Visionary

San Jose Home, which began its journey on August 17, 2023, was born from the vision of Fr. Jose Varickamackal, a dedicated priest of the Diocese of Kanjirapally. His unwavering commitment to serving the elderly led him to contribute his patrimony and personal funds towards this noble cause.

Currently residing at San Jose Home, Fr. Varickamackal continues to guide the institution's progress while serving as the Spiritual Director, offering his services free of charge. His presence and ongoing dedication ensure that the home maintains its founding principles of compassionate care and spiritual guidance for all residents.

OUR LOCATION

How to Reach Us

Our Location

Cheenikuzhy is a rural village situated in the Udubannoor Panchayat, Thodupuzha Taluk, on the western side of Idukki District in Kerala State, approximately 19 kilometers from Thodupuzha.

From Thodupuzha
  • Take the bus route through:
    • • Muthalakodom
    • • Karimannoor
    • • Udumbannoor
    • • Parekkavala
  • From Parekkavala, take either of the two roads leading to San Jose Home (3.5 km)
From Kattappana & Idukki
  • Take the Idukki-Moolamattom Road
  • Reach Paaramada Junction (4 km before Kulamavu Dam)
  • Take the tarred road on the right to San Jose Home (14 km from Paramada)

San Jose Home is surrounded by lush greenery and hills, appealing to those who appreciate solitude and nature.
